About Me

Hi, I’m Anna — a full-stack developer with a curious mind, creative drive, and a love for clean, meaningful code. Ever since I was a child, I dreamed of becoming a programmer. That dream shaped my education: I studied mathematics and computer science, building a solid foundation in logic and problem-solving. But life had its own twists — my first professional chapter led me into SEO and digital marketing. Over the years, I gained deep insight into how people interact with digital spaces.

Still, something inside kept calling me back to development. And eventually, I listened. Because you can’t cheat destiny — and it turned out my childhood instinct was right all along. Today, I combine my technical background with a creative approach to building user-friendly, fast, and responsive websites and apps. I enjoy working on high-load projects, intuitive interfaces, and anything that demands both structure and imagination.

When I’m not coding, I’m probably playing piano, skiing through snowy trails, diving into photography, or sipping a warm drink while sketching new ideas. I believe in lifelong learning and enjoy working with people who value clarity, creativity, and collaboration. Let’s build something great together.
